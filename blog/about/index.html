About this blog

This blog includes notes on programming tools and music-related topics.

You will find posts related to music technology and perception and notes on programming. More specifically about Emacs, Orgmode, Arch Linux & MacOS, python, MATLAB, SuperCollider and more.

The blog serves mostly as a personal notebook. If you find anything useful in here I will be more than happy.
