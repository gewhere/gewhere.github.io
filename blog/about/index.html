About this blog

This blog includes notes on programming tools and sound-related topics.

You will find posts related to sound technology, cognition and notes on programming. More specifically about Emacs, Orgmode, Arch Linux & MacOS, python, MATLAB, SuperCollider and more.

The blog serves mostly as a personal notebook. If you find anything useful in here I will be more than happy.

About me

I am PhD student at the division of Applied Acoustics at Chalmers Universtity of Technology. I am pursuing doctoral studies in building acoustics with a particular focus in human bodily motion.
